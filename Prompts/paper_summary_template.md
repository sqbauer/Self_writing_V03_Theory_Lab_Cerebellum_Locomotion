# Paper Summary Template

This template is used to convert literature PDFs into structured summaries that the theory lab can reason over.

---

## Citation
Full citation.

## Core Question
What question is the paper trying to answer?

## Methods (short)
Key experimental or computational methods used.

## Key Findings
Bullet points of the main results.

## Mechanistic Insight
What mechanism does the paper suggest?

## Evidence Relevant to Current Theory
Does this support or contradict any hypotheses in the hypothesis registry?

## Variables Measured
Which locomotor or neural variables were measured?

Examples:
- step length
- step timing
- Purkinje cell firing
- granule cell activity
- climbing fiber signals

## Predictions Implied
If the paper’s interpretation is correct, what predictions follow?

## Weaknesses / Open Questions
Limitations or unanswered questions.

## Relevance Score
High / Medium / Low for cerebellar locomotion theory.
